Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 19, 2026

PiggyBank ("the App") respects your privacy.

Information We Collect

The App does not collect, store, or share personal information such as your name, email address, phone number, or location.

Camera Permission

The App requests access to the device camera only to scan barcodes and product codes. Camera data is processed only on your device and is not transmitted anywhere.

Local Data Storage

The App stores product history, cart data, and settings locally on your device only.

Internet Access

The App does not require an internet connection for its core functionality.

Third-Party Services

The App does not use advertising networks, analytics services, or third-party tracking tools.

Children's Privacy

The App is designed for general audiences and does not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Data Security

Because all data is stored locally on the device, users remain in control of their information.

Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Any changes will be published on this page.
